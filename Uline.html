Uline UX

Project highlights from my time at Uline working as a UX Designer.

At Uline, I create simple solutions to complex problems for e-commerce and internal tools. While I'm limited in what I can share due to confidentiality, these summaries highlight the process, collaboration, and impact of my work.

Careers Redesign

I led the redesign of Uline’s careers site to improve the applicant experience. I worked with a UX Researcher to uncover user pain points and ran multiple ideations to align stakeholders on solutions.

I designed and presented out an updated look and feel, then created reusable templates to ensure a consistent experience across the application journey.

Creating a Design System

I led the effort to modernize our Sales tool and streamline the design-to-development handoff. My team audited the app and documented all UI elements and use cases. I redesigned components to cover every scenario, then partnered with IT to build them into code.

Once complete, I championed the system across teams, showing how it improved efficiency and consistency.
